General information

1. Title of Dataset: Fungal pathogens and nitrogen addition alter community spatial stability via different mechanisms

2. Date of data collection:  2023-August

3. Geographic location of data collection: Qinghai Haibei National Field Research Station of Alpine Grassland Ecosystem of Northwest Institute of Plateau Biology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in Menyuan County, Qinghai Province, People's Republic of China (101° 19’E, 37° 36’ N; 3215 m above sea level)

4. Information about funding sources that supported the collection of the data: National Natural Science Foundation of China (Grants No. 31830009), and the Fundamental Research Funds in Hainan University (KYQD (ZR) -20081).

5. Variable List

    Plot: the identity of each plot
    Block: the identity of each block
    Treatment: control, fungal pathogen exclusion,  nitrogen addition, fungal pathogen exclusion & nitrogen   addition
    Mean_Biomass: mean value of community biomass for three subplot within each plot
    SD_Biomass: standard deviation of community biomass for three subplot within each plot
    Community_Spatial_Stability:  spatial stability of community biomass
    Species_Asychrony: asynchrony  for three subplot within each plot
    Species_Stability: mean stability of each species weighted by the species relative abundance  for three subplot within each plot
Mean_SR: mean value of community species richness for three subplot within each plot
Mean_Beta_Jaccard：mean value of community beta diversity (Jaccard dissimilarity index) for three subplot within each plot
Mean_Shannon: mean value of community Shannon's diversity index for three subplot within each plot
Mean_Beta_Bray：mean value of community beta diversity (Bray-Curtis dissimilarity index) for three subplot within each plot




    (The empty means no data.)
